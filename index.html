About the Barangay

Barangay Matindeg is a dynamic community located in Cuyapo, Nueva Ecija. Dedicated to improving the lives of its residents through transparent governance, community programs, and sustainable development. We aim to foster unity, security, and continuous progress.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To effectively address the desire and needs of people by educating and empowering them through geniune public service and responsible local governance.

Vision

Cuyapo is a strategic, premier agri-eco tourism center in Nueva Ecija, with a healthy, empowered and Godly citizenry flourishing in a safe, adaptive and livable community, enjoying a clean and preserved environment with a balanced ecology, while thriving in a progressive, sustainable and competitive economy, that is supported by a well-planned, accessible and resilient infrastructure and served by a proactive, collaborative, well-equipped, and highly-motivated local government.
